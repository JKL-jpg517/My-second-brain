## Inverse of a matrix
- def: $A$: square matrix  *inverse matrix*: a square matrix that enables $A^{-1}A=I$  /$AA^{-1}=I$ $\iff$ $A^{-1}$ undoes $A$ does
- ***Not all matricies have inverses*** 
- concept connections: [Inverse&independent&rank&determinant](../../../../Concepts/Inverse&independent&rank&determinant&pibots.md)
>[!tip]- two perspective of inverse matricies
>1. determine the inverse by $AA^{-1}=I$
>2. determine it by undoing the operation![introduction-to-linear-algebra-6nbsped-1733146679-9781733146678_compress, p.52](Academic/Sources/数学/教辅/introduction-to-linear-algebra-6nbsped-1733146679-9781733146678_compress.pdf#page=62&rect=102,454,415,500)

## The inverse of mutiplication
$(AB)^{-1}=B^{-1}A^{-1}$
$(ABC)^{-1}=C^{-1}B^{-1}A^{-1}$ 
- operartion: $AB$  $B$ operates first; then $A$$\implies$  inverse $A^{-1}$ goes first
## The inverse of elimination matrix
- for $E$: does not reveal the elimination formula directly ==since the elimination steps may effect other elimination steps==

- for $E^{-1}$ : multiplied in reverse order: the rows will not interfere with each other!
>[!example]-
>![introduction-to-linear-algebra-6nbsped-1733146679-9781733146678_compress, p.53](Academic/Sources/数学/教辅/introduction-to-linear-algebra-6nbsped-1733146679-9781733146678_compress.pdf#page=63&rect=79,409,420,464)
>why not interfere? 处理上层为 0 的矩阵在最左侧，逐渐向右往下；而$E$中处理上层为 0 的矩阵在最右侧 逐渐向左往下；矩阵乘法得知$E^{-1}$的系数不相互影响

$\implies$ $A=E^{-1}U=LU$
if we want the diagnals of $U$ to be 1; use diagnal matrix $D$ 
$A=LDU$
