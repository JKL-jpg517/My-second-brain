### Rank
The rank of $A$ is the number of pivots
$\implies$ the final matrix $R$ will have $r$ nonzero rows
every free column is a combination of earlier piviot columns!
$\implies$ nullspace has $n-r$ dimentions
$rank(AB)\leq rank(A)$
$rank(AB)\leq rank(B)$
